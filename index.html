India accounts for close to 1.6 million premature deaths due to indoor and outdoor air pollution.

Air pollution is an urgent public health crisis which requires systemic and nation-wide solutions. We need a time bound National Clean Air Action Plan which includes emergency measures and plans for reducing emissions from all sources of pollution.

Our present and future is threatened from this serious problem. You don’t have to imagine what will happen if children breathe toxic and poisonous air everyday, because it is happening right now!
